What I help with
One resource, every type of applicant

Whether you're a high school senior applying for the first time or a community college student going through the transfer process, I help you put together the strongest application possible.

UC Personal Insight Questions

All 8 PIQ prompts — brainstorming, drafting, revising. I know what UC readers are actually looking for.

Common App Essay

The 650-word personal statement. Picking your angle, structuring your story, and refining your voice.

Transfer Personal Statement

The UC transfer personal insight questions are a different beast. I help community college students tell their story in a way that resonates with transfer admissions.

School Selection

Building a balanced list of UCs based on your GPA, intended major, and goals — reaches, targets, and safeties for both freshmen and transfers.

Application Review

Full application audit — activities list, honors, additional comments, and overall packaging. First-year or transfer, I cover it all.

Timeline Planning

Custom roadmap so you're not scrambling at the deadline. Different timelines for first-year vs transfer applicants — I know both.

1-on-1 Coaching

Live sessions via Zoom to work through essays together, answer questions, and keep you on track — no matter where you are in the process.

Major & Path Guidance

Not sure what to major in or which path makes sense for you? I help you think through your options and position yourself accordingly in your application.

Chances Assessment

Honest, realistic evaluation of your profile — GPA, coursework, activities — and where you stand for your target schools.
